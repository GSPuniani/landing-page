About the Product

The Tennis Roomba is a tennis ball collector for the 21st century. It is a compact, battery-powered machine operated by our smartphone app. The app lets you drive the Tennis Roomba like an RC car using the built-in camera and allows you to raise and lower the basket for easy access to the tennis balls.

Intuitive

Easily pair your phone to the Tennis Roomba to pilot it around the court. Our app makes it easy and fun to steer the Tennis Roomba.

Convenient

The Tennis Roomba is portable, so it's easy to carry off the tennis court into your car. It can also tolerate up to 8 hours of continuous use.

Affordable

Spend more time playing and less time milling around the court to collect all the tennis balls.
